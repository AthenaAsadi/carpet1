Haj Khalili Carpet Store with Over 40 Years of Experience

Haj Khalili Store, with more than 40 years of outstanding experience in the production, preparation, buying, and selling of carpets, is one of the oldest and most reputable centers in this industry across Iran. This establishment proudly offers carpets of unparalleled quality at reasonable prices and has always strived to showcase the artistry of the distinguished artisans from Kerman. Our carpets are not only symbols of Iranian beauty and craftsmanship, but also represent the history, culture, and authenticity of this land.

At Haj Khalili Store, we believe that every carpet tells a unique story, and its weave springs from the heart of Iran's history and culture. Therefore, we aim to meet the needs and preferences of each customer by offering specialized consultations and diverse colorings.

Authenticity, quality, and art are at the core of our activities, and as representatives of Iran's carpet industry, we strive to contribute to the promotion of this valuable art. Our goal is not only to sell products but also to create a unique experience for our customers when choosing and purchasing carpets.
